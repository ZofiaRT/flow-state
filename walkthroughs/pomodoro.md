# Stay Focused with Pomodoro

Working continuously for hours is a fast track to burnout. Flow-State includes a built-in **Pomodoro Timer** to help you pace your work.

* **Focus Intervals:** Work in structured, uninterrupted blocks of time which are adjusted based on your focus state.
* **Regular Breaks:** Flow-State will remind you to step away from the screen, stretch, and rest your eyes.

You can start, pause, or configure your Pomodoro intervals directly from the command palette or the status bar!
