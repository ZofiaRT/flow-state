# Reduce Tab Clutter

Having dozens of open tabs increases cognitive load and makes it harder to find what you actually need. 

Flow-State monitors your open editors and **keeps track of inactive tabs**. If you have too many files open that you haven't looked at or edited in a while, Flow-State will alert you. 

It provides a quick way for you to **review and select specific inactive tabs to close**, ensuring your workspace remains clean and relevant to your current task. 

*Keep your environment tidy, and your mind will follow!*
