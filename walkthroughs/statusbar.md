# Keep Your Flow Optimal

Flow-State runs quietly in the background to protect you from burnout. 

Look at the bottom right of your VS Code window for the **Flow State** indicator. 

Hovering over this indicator gives you a quick health-check of your current environment, including:
- **Cognitive Load** - your code complexity, current alerts and suggestions to mitigate them.
- **Reviewer Cognitive Load** - statistics and alerts for your staged files.
